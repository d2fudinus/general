1. Introduction

POS Simple Modern ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile Point of Sale application designed to help manage retail business operations including product management, sales transactions, and reporting.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

  • Store Information: store name, address, and phone number (stored locally on your device)
  • Product Data: product names, prices, categories, and stock information (stored locally on your device)
  • Transaction Data: sales records, payment methods, and transaction history (stored locally on your device)

2.2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: app usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data (via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics)
  • Advertising Data: ad interaction data (via Google AdMob)

2.3. Location Information

We DO NOT collect or track your precise location data.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1. Local Data Storage

  • All business data (products, categories, transactions, and settings) is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database.
  • This data is not transmitted to our servers or third parties.
  • Data remains private and under your control.

3.2. Third-Party Services

  1. Firebase (Google)
    • Firebase Analytics: collects usage statistics to improve app performance
    • Firebase Crashlytics: collects crash reports to fix bugs
    • Firebase Remote Config: delivers app configuration updates
    • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  2. Google AdMob
    • Displays advertisements within the app
    • Collects advertising identifiers and ad interaction data
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  3. Google Play Services
    • In-App Purchase functionality
    • In-App Review functionality
    • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: all your business data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: we do not sync your data to cloud servers
  • Device Security: your data security depends on your device's security measures
  • No Account Required: the app does not require registration or login

5. Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your business data with anyone
  • Upload your transaction data to external servers

Third-party services (Firebase, AdMob) may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: view all data stored in the app
  • Delete: clear all app data through device settings
  • Control: disable analytics in app settings (if available)
  • Opt-out: opt out of personalized advertising through device settings

7. Data Retention

  • Data is retained locally on your device until you delete it.
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.
  • Analytics data retention follows Firebase's data retention policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Advertising

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

10. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: to store local database and app data
  • Bluetooth: to connect to Bluetooth thermal printers (optional)
  • Internet: to display ads and send analytics
  • Network State: to check internet connectivity
  • AD_ID: for advertising purposes (Android 13+)

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
